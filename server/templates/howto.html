How To Play

Back

Each day at midnight Eastern Time, a 5-letter word is selected at random from the dictionary. You have until the next midnight to guess the word. Use the on-screen keyboard provided to enter letters. You get six tries. The game will provide hints after each guess as follows:
Grey background means the letter hasn't been tried yet.
Black background means the letter is not in the word.
Orange background means the letter is in the word but in the wrong place.
Green background means the letter is in the word and in the right place.
